Who is The Punisher?

The Punisher is an antihero created by writer Gerry Conway and artists John Romita sr. and Ross Andru for Marvel Comics. He first appeared in issue #129 of The Amazing Spider Man which was published in Febuary 1974. Over the last 49 years this character has appeared in 2779 issues and has expanded into video games, TV shows, and major motion pictures.
